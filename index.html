PLM-Net

Perception Latency Mitigation Network for Vision-Based Lateral Control of Autonomous Vehicles

Authors: Aws Khalil*, Jane Doe*, Prof. X

📄 Abstract

This project addresses the challenge of perception latency in autonomous vehicle control. PLM-Net predicts future control actions to compensate for delays in vision-based systems, improving steering accuracy and trajectory stability.
